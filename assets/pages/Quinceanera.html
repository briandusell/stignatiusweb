St. Ignatius Parish Quinceañera Policies and Fees

Parish Requirements

St. Ignatius parish requires that those celebrating Quinceañeras be registered members of the parish and have received the sacraments of baptism, first communion, and confirmation.

Quinceañera celebrations at St. Ignatius are presided over by a deacon. Our parish deacons are Deacon Rogelio Soto and Deacon Raul Mora.

Quinceañera celebration dates and times must be finalized with the parish office and entered into the master calendar. Determining dates and times with the officiating deacon does not finalize the celebration date and time. It must be confirmed by Mercedes Mac Laughlin - Family faith life coordinator.

The fee for a Quinceañera celebration is $300.00 for registered families.

Quinceañera Music Fees and Policies

The base fee for Quinceañera music is $150.00 which includes the services of our Director of Music who will both play piano or organ and cantor for the ceremony. The Coro Hispano may be hired for an additional fee of $100.00. A professional vocalist may be hired as soloist for an additional $150.00.

Outside musicians may be hired by the family to provide musical accompaniment for the music in place of the parish musicians.
