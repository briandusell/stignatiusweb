St. Ignatius Parish Capital Campaign

Support the future of our parish. In March of 2012, St. Ignatius launched a multi-year capital fundraising campaign with a goal of raising $500,000 to enhance our parish ministries and continue the maintenance and restoration of our magnificent church. To date, (June 2013), we have raised nearly $400,000 toward our $500,000 goal.
