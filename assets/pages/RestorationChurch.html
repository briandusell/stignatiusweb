Church and Chapel Restoration

For the past twenty years, work to restore the Church, Chapel, and school has been ongoing. The continued support of St. Ignatius parishioners and friends has been crucial to making this work come to fruition. From small donations to major grants, to volunteer labor and expertise, help comes in many forms. Please support our restoration efforts.

Some highlights of this work include:

The restoration of the Chapel of Our Lady of the Angels

The restoration of the Church Ceiling

The restoration of the Church Vestibule

Installation of a new line array sound system for the Church and Chapel

The renovation of the Canteen

The renovation of the Holy Name Room

The ongoing restoration of the Kilgen Pipe Organ

The restoration of the Deagan Tower Chimes

The restoration of the Holy Family and Patriotic stained glass windows

The ongoing tuck pointing of the Church and Bell Tower

New windows in the school buildings
