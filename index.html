"Catholicism" Faith Formation Program

Thursday evenings from 7:30-9 p.m. Canteen Register now for the "Catholicism" Faith Formation Program. This program begins on Thursday, January 30, 2014 and continues Thursday evenings from 7:30-9:00 p.m. through May 8, 2014 in the canteen. At the heart of the series will be the outstanding video documentary "Catholicism" hosted by Fr. Robert Barron. Registration is required. The cost is $30.00 per family. For more information, please contact Kathy Morris at is@stignatiuschurch.org or by calling the rectory office at (773) 764-5936.

Knights of Columbus Spaghetti Dinner

Saturday, February 22, 5 - 10 p.m. Holy Name Room and Canteen The University Council #1687 Knights of Columbus presents our annual Spaghetti Dinner Saturday, February 22, 2014. Social Hour in the Canteen 5 p.m.; Dinner is served at 5:45 p.m.; Entertainment begins at 6:15 p.m.; 50/50 raffles; Door Prizes; Live Entertainment; Ticket includes all the spaghetti you can eat, one drink, bread, and salad. Water, soda, wine and beer are available for purchase in the Canteen. Advance tickets are available at the St. Ignatius Rectory and after Sunday masses.
Ticket prices:
In advance: $10.00 Adult, $5.00 Children
At the door: $12.00 Adult, $7.00 Children
$30 for a family (2 adults, and 2 or more children under 12)

Market Day

Pickup Saturday, March 1, 12-1 p.m. Holy Name Room St. Ignatius participates in the Market Day monthly grocery fundraising program. A portion of the profits go to assist parish programs. Order food items online through Market Day's convenient website and pick up your purchases from the Holy Name Room once a month. The next Market Day Pick up is 12-1 p.m. in the Holy Name Room, Saturday, October 26

St. Patrick's Day Party

Saturday, March 8, 2014 St. Ignatius Gymnasium Please attend the St. Ignatius Parish annual St. Pat's Party. Held in the school gym at 1300 West Loyola Avenue, the doors open at 6:00 p.m. and Dinner is served at 6:30 p.m. The evening's entertainment features the McGowens, the World Champion Trinity Irish Dancers, and the Shannon Rovers. The meal is a traditional Irish Corned Beef Dinner complete with a St. Joseph sweet table. Advance tickets are available at the St. Ignatius Rectory and after Sunday masses.
Ticket prices:
Adults $20 ($25 at the door)
Seniors $15
Children 12 and under $10

St. Ignatius Parish Capital Campaign

Contribute Now Support the future of our parish. Please contribute to the Celebrating Our Faith Building Our Future St. Ignatius Parish Capital Campaign. In March of 2012, St. Ignatius launched a multi-year capital fund raising campaign with a goal of raising $500,000 to enhance our parish ministries and continue the maintenance and restoration of our magnificent church. To date, (June 2013), we have raised nearly $400,000 toward our $500,000 goal.
