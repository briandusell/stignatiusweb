COMING EVENTS

Market Day

Pickup Saturday, November 23, 12-1 p.m. Holy Name Room St. Ignatius participates in the Market Day monthly grocery fundraising program. A portion of the profits go to assist parish programs. Order food items online through Market Day's convenient website and pick up your purchases from the Holy Name Room once a month. The next Market Day Pick up is 12-1 p.m. in the Holy Name Room, Saturday, October 26

Saturday Mass Time Change to 4 p.m.

Saturday afternoons at 4 p.m. Chapel The start time for the St. Ignatius Saturday Vigil Mass is changed to 4 p.m. during the winter months. This mass meets in the Chapel every Saturday. Confessions are likewise moved to 3:30 - 4:00 p.m. in the Chapel immediately preceding the mass.

Choral Concert - Ecclesia Choir

Sunday November 24th, 3 p.m. St. Ignatius Chapel The Ecclesia Choir of Chicago
Timothy Woods, director
Music by the Polyphonic Masters in honor of Joseph M. Woods. Works by Palestrina, Victoria, Lassus, Willaert, Rosselli, as well as the" Missa Deus Sempiterne" by Chicago composer Kevin Allen. The program also includes the choral favorites "Ave Verum" by Mozart and "Panis Angelicus" by Franck. Tim Woods, conductor; Sarah Maletta guest conductor.
Tickets have a suggested donation price of $15.
$10 for children 12 and under.

Advent Spiritual Retreat

Friday-Sunday, December 20-22 Marytown Retreat and Conference Center
4600 West Park Avenue, Libertyville, IL 60048 St. Ignatius resident priest Fr. Patrick McAteer, SJ is directing an Advent spiritual retreat entitled "Mary's Christmas Gift to the World". The cost is $140.00 for retreatants who will be staying at the Marytown Center. The cost is $50.00 for commuter retreatants. All rooms at Marytown are double occupancy. Meals are included through Sunday morning brunch. For more information please contact Fr. McAteer at pmacsj@stignatiuschurch.org or view the retreat page of Fr. McAteer's website.
