Paschal Triduum and Easter Sunday Thursday, April 17-Sunday, April 20 Main Church Please join us for the celebration of the Paschal Triduum beginning Thursday evening April 17 with the Holy Thursday mass at 7:30 p.m.. There are two Good Friday services on Friday, April 18: in English at 3 p.m. and in Spanish at 7:30 p.m. The Easter Vigil is Saturday, April 19 at 8 p.m. Easter Sunday services are at 8 a.m., 10 a.m. in English and 12 p.m. in Spanish. There will be no 6 p.m. mass on Easter Sunday.

Market Day Pickup Saturday, May 3, 12-1 p.m. Holy Name Room St. Ignatius participates in the Market Day monthly grocery fundraising program. A portion of the profits go to assist parish programs. Order food items online through Market Day's convenient website and pick up your purchases from the Holy Name Room once a month. The next Market Day Pick up is 12-1 p.m. in the Holy Name Room, Saturday, March 29.

St. Ignatius Parish Capital Campaign Contribute Now Support the future of our parish. Please contribute to the Celebrating Our Faith Building Our Future St. Ignatius Parish Capital Campaign. In March of 2012, St. Ignatius launched a multi-year capital fund raising campaign with a goal of raising $500,000 to enhance our parish ministries and continue the maintenance and restoration of our magnificent church. To date, (June 2013), we have raised nearly $400,000 toward our $500,000 goal.
