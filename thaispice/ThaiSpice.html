Our Story
Return to Top ⤴

Sawadee! Opened in 1993, Thai Spice Restaurant is known for its delightful and intimate dining experience. Thai Spice serves excellent northern Thai food, with quality and taste to meet your liking. Thai Spice uses only the freshest ingredients and spices and cooks them with our traditional methods that have been handed down through generations. The consistency of our flavor and service is noteworthy. Well, its because we've had the same incredible chef since the day we opened for business! Each entree is individually prepared. Please allow some time while Thai Spice creates your generously proportioned meals.

Directions
Return to Top ⤴

Thai Spice Restaurant is located at 1320 West Devon Avenue between Lakewood and Wayne avenues on the north side of the street in the Rogers Park neighborhood of Chicago. The restaurant is two and a half blocks west of Sheridan Road and the north shore campus of Loyola University Chicago.

Thai Spice Restaurant is also easily accessible via mass transit being two blocks south and two blocks west of the Loyola Red Line "EL" stop. For easy walking from the Loyola "EL" station, walk south to Devon Avenue and then west two and a half blocks to the restaurant.
