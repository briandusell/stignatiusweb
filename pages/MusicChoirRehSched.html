St. Ignatius Choir Rehearsal Schedules

Parish choir rehearsal is every Tuesday from 7:30 p.m.-9:30 p.m. in the choir rehearsal room in the main church.

Coro de Niños practices Saturday mornings from 10:30-11:30 a.m. in the choir rehearsal room during the choir season.

The fall 2013 Coro de Niños season will begin Saturday, August 24, 2013. Please contact Dr. Brian DuSell if you would like for your child to become a member of the Coro De Niños.

Coro Hispano rehearsals are on Sunday mornings at 11:30 a.m. immediately preceding the 12 p.m. mass.

For all rehearsals in the rehearsal room please enter through the doors on the south side of the main church (the Loyola Avenue entrance).
