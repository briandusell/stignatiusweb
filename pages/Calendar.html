Weekly Calendar

Monday

The parish staff weekly staff meeting is from 1:00-2:30 p.m. each Monday. The Circulo de Oración, (Circle of Prayer-a Spanish charismatic prayer group) meets in the Holy Name Room, at 7:00 p.m..

Tuesday

Parish Choir rehearsal every Tuesday from September - June in the rehearsal room at 7:30 p.m.. People attending choir rehearsals should enter through the Loyola Avenue entrance on the south side of church.

Wednesday

Spanish Baptism classes are held in the Holy Name Room at 7:00 p.m. the Wednesday before each baptism. Generally this means that there are classes held on the first, second, and fourth Wednesdays of the month. For more information about these classes please call the rectory.

The regular publication submission day for the parish bulletin is Wednesday. The deadline for bullentin submissions is noon on Wednesday. For holiday bulletins, the deadlines are set even earlier. Please call the rectory for more information.

Thursday

The Grupo del Rosario prays the Rosary in Spanish in the Chapel at 7pm every Thursday.

Friday

Eucharistic Adoration from 8:30 a.m. -3 p.m. on the first Friday of every month. The Circulo de Oración meets in the Holy Name Room every Friday at 7:00 p.m.. Eucharistic Adoration in the Chapel every Friday from 7-9 p.m..

Saturday

Daily Mass in the Chapel at 8 a.m., The Legion of Mary meets immediately following the Saturday morning mass. CCD classes are on Saturday mornings from 9 - 10:30 a.m. in the church and school. Please see the CCD calendar for more details. Coro de Ninos rehearsals are on Saturday mornings at 10:30 a.m. during the choir season. Confession in the chapel is available for 30 minutes prior to the beginning of the Saturday Vigil mass., Saturday Vigil Mass at 5 p.m. (4 p.m. in the winter months) in the chapel.

Sunday

Coffee and Doughnut Socials following 8 a.m., 10 a.m., and 12 p.m. Masses. Come out and meet your fellow parishioners after Mass!
