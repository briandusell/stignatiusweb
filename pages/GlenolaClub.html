Glenola Club of Loyola Center

The Glenola Club is a non-profit organization, incorporated under the State of Illinois for social, civic and educational and benevolent activities.

Incorporated September 23, 1921

Invocation

O Holy Spirit, we implore you to inspire and guide the members of this club that their work may redound to the greater honor and glory of God. Amen.

Mrs. T. J. Byrne - 1922
