"Mary's Christmas Gift to the World"

An Advent spiritual retreat to be held at Marytown Retreat and Conference Center
4600 West Park Avenue
Libertyville, IL 60048
Weekend, December 20-22, 2013

Mary's Christmas Gift to the world began with her conception of Jesus Christ the Son of God at the Annunciation when she made her "fiat" to the Angel Gabriel: "Let it be done to me according to your work." It concluded on earth at Calvary, with the last words of Jesus Christ on the Cross, "Now it is finished." Like Mary, Jesus let the will of the Father be done to him in his life.

The total assent of the Virgin was the Child's spiritual womb. Only then did the Mother's body also become the bearing womb. From the moment that the angel speaks to her, her expectation becomes different. She goes from the Old Testament to New Testament expectation. Before she had waited for the promised fullness: from now on that which she awaits, the promise, is already within her as fulfillment.

The material for reflection on this weekend retreat will be taken from Marian texts in the Christian Bible and from the book, Handmaid of the Lord, by Adrienne Von Speyr. Adrienne Von Speyr (20 September 1902 - 17 September 1967) was a Swiss medical doctor and the author of over 60 books on spirituality and theology.
